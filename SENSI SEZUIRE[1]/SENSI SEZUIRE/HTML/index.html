Meet Sensi Seizure — a compassionate health companion designed to help people living with seizures track triggers, stay on routine, and alert loved ones when it matters.

Built by AI Global Networks in partnership with lived experience, Sensi Seizure brings together reminders, trigger journaling, and smart guidance to support patients and caregivers—without overwhelming them. Simple. Private. Always there when you need it.

In active development

Our first release focuses on daily routines (medication and hydration reminders), quick logging of possible triggers (sleep, stress, light), and optional shareable reports for clinicians or caregivers. Future iterations will explore proactive insights and secure, opt‑in alerting.
